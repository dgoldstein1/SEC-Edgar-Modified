         CISCO SYSTEMS COMPLETES ACQUISITION OF KOMODO TECHNOLOGY, INC.

     SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Sept. 25, 2000--Cisco Systems, Inc.,
today announced it has completed the acquisition of Komodo Technology, Inc. of
Los Gatos, California.

     On July 25, 2000, Cisco announced a definitive agreement to acquire Komodo,
a leading developer of Voice-over-IP (VoIP) devices that allow analog telephones
to place calls over IP-based networks. This acquisition is being accounted for
using purchase accounting.
