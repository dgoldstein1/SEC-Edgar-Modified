CISCO SYSTEMS COMPLETES ACQUISITION OF VOVIDA NETWORKS

SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nov 9, 2000--Cisco Systems, Inc., today
announced it has completed the acquisition of privately held Vovida Networks,
Inc. of San Jose, California.

On Sept. 28, 2000, Cisco announced a definitive agreement to acquire Vovida
Networks, a communications software provider and supplier of networking
protocols. This acquisition is being accounted for using purchase accounting.

ABOUT CISCO SYSTEMS

Cisco Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ: CSCO) is the worldwide leader in networking for the
Internet. News and information are available at www.cisco.com.
