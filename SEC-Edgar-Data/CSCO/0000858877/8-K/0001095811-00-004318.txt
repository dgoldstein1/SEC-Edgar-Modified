CISCO SYSTEMS COMPLETES ACQUISITION OF IPCELL TECHNOLOGIES

SAN JOSE, Calif.--(BUSINESS WIRE)--Nov. 3, 2000--Cisco Systems, Inc., today
announced it has completed the acquisition of IPCell Technologies, Inc. of
Richardson, Texas.

On Sept. 28, 2000, Cisco announced a definitive agreement to acquire IPCell
Technologies, a provider of software for broadband access networks combining
Internet Protocol (IP) and telephony services. This acquisition is being
accounted for using purchase accounting.

ABOUT CISCO SYSTEMS

Cisco Systems, Inc. (NASDAQ: CSCO) is the worldwide leader in networking for the
Internet. News and information are available at www.cisco.com.
